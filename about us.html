ABOUT US

COMIC CANVAS IS GOOD AND EASY WEBSITE TO EXPLORE YOUR DRAWING AND STORY TELLING TALENT. WE ESTABLISHED IN 2020, AND WE HAVE 5 BRANCHES. OUR STAFF IS WELL TRAINED AND WELL MANNERED.

1.Our Mission:​

COMIC CANVAS was founded with a mission to "entertain, inspire, and foster a sense of community through the power of comics.". We believe in the ability of comics to transcend boundaries and connect people from different walks of life.

2.Our approach:

At COMIC CANVAS, we take pride in "curating a diverse range of comics that cater to various genres and interests". We believe in fostering a supportive community for both creators and readers, where creativity knows no bounds. ​
